Administrative Automation

Eliminate manual charging ledgers entirely.

Managing EV lines manually creates heavy overhead. Committee volunteers or parking staff waste hours copying meter codes, adjusting slab calculations, and chasing drivers for receipt collections.

The EBEE Dashboard coordinates the complete back-end loops automatically. From UPI/card payment collections to slab splits calculations, we route paybacks directly to your account with zero administrative workload.

Proven Case Studies

Software Impact.

Case Study: Residential

Eliminating accounting disputes

A 200-apartment housing society manually compiled spreadsheet readings for 15 EV slots. Arguments over exact slab splits wasted hours of committee time. Integrating EBEE Dashboard with their ledger automated calculations, billing flat owners directly on invoice cycles.

Outcome: Split billing disputes cut to zero. Volunteers saved 12 hours monthly.

Case Study: Commercial

Dynamic tariff yield boosts

An office park developer installed 30 EV bays. Slotted terminals stood idle during mornings. Implementing dynamic off-peak rates (20% tariff discounts from 9 AM - 12 PM) flattened grid loads and increased total charger utilization.

Outcome: Morning utilization index rose by 40%.
