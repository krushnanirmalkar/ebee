How MOVABLE CHARGER Works

MOVABLE CHARGER is our mobile sequential charging layer.
Roll, plug, and sequentially charge multiple EVs on a single circuit.

Deploy Unit

Valet unlocks the MOVABLE CHARGER mobile unit and rolls it to the parked EV's slot.

Scan QR

Driver scans the QR code casing to launch the WhatsApp chat screen.

Pay & Start

Driver selects their power bundle and pays directly inside the chat interface.

Connect Cable

Valet plugs the retractable cable into the EV's actual side charging port.

Session Active

Fast 22kW charging initiates under the Smart DB load management loop.
