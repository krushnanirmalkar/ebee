Deployments

Case Study: Residential

Greenwood residency load balance

Greenwood society operated under a strict 45kW municipal transformer cap. Installing standard private chargers meant risking total grid failure when more than six vehicles charged simultaneously.

Outcome: Deployed a single Smart DB, expanding slot capacities to 12. Spared Greenwood from a ₹4.5 Lakh transformer rebuild.

Case Study: Commercial

Nexus Mall peak load balancing

Nexus Mall basement parking needed to maintain 15 chargers. During weekend shopping peaks, elevators and cooling loops drew massive power, causing localized voltage fluctuations.

Outcome: Smart DB coordinates load parameters between air conditioning loops and EV lines. Voltage dropouts cut to zero.
