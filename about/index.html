Our Mission

We simplify EV charging infrastructure.

EBEECHARGE was founded with a single objective: to remove the friction from parking lot electrification, creating sustainable value for properties and vehicle owners alike.

Architecting India's EV Transition

As EV adoption gains rapid traction, properties face a major challenge. Standard electrical grids were never built to support dozens of fast-charging vehicles drawing power at the same time. Setting up individual lines creates chaos and breaker overloads.

EBEECHARGE bridges this gap. By designing intelligent hardware panels that balance power dynamically, and deploying movable charging terminals that eliminate parking bay lockouts, we maximize grid capacity. We convert standard parking spaces into smart grid components with zero complexity.
